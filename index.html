SERVICES

What we do is very simple; how we do it is slightly more complicated.

The results are beautifully laundered sheets and pillowcases; fluffy towelling and robes; immaculately presented tablecloths and napkins; white chefs wear – the complete 5* service.

RFID (Radio Frequency Identification)

Royal Jersey has invested in and introduced RFID chipping to their rental linen and towelling; this is a unique service which is also available to hotels with their own luxury linen/towelling; enabling accurate and efficient stock checks and the ability to track the usage of each individual item.

ENVIRONMENT

Our Commitment to the Environment

A laundry is undoubtedly a high user of water; gas and electric; however, Royal Jersey is committed to reducing their impact on the environment.

The plant machinery is new and energy efficient, we wash in three continuous batch washers, each processing 50kg of linen per minute, using 85% less water than a conventional washer extractor.

With the recent installation of a water recovery plant, we recycle 80% of our water, as the recycled water is already heated, we use less energy; therefore, reducing our carbon footprint by 600 tons per year.

HISTORY

Celebrating our centenary in 2015, the business is owned by Chris Westcott the grandson of its founder Walter, who in 1915 bought Royal Jersey operating it as a domestic laundry.

Today’s Royal Jersey is a world away from the set up in 1915; with a recent £3million machinery investment and relocation to larger premises in 2009, we are immensely proud of our history and our consistent achievement; most businesses do not survive 100 years because commercial business life in the 21st Century is stressful and highly competitive.
